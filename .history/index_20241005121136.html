About Us

About Restaurant

Nestled in the heart of Kingstown, St. Vincent, we invite you to escape the ordinary and dive into a paradise of taste. Our vibrant atmosphere, refreshing cocktails, and mouthwatering wing options make us the perfect spot for friends, families, and wing enthusiasts alike. Whether you’re here to catch the game or just enjoy a sunny afternoon, we’ve got you covered. Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey that brings the spirit of the tropics right to your table!

At SweetSugaWingz, we believe that every bite should transport you to a tropical paradise. Founded by Shannon & Tedra, our passion for delicious food and a fun atmosphere led to the creation of this unique dining experience. We pride ourselves on using only the freshest ingredients, with an emphasis on bold flavors inspired by island cuisine. Our wings are crafted to perfection, with a variety of sauces ranging from sweet and tangy to spicy and smoky. Beyond wings, we offer a selection of tropical-inspired cocktails and sides, ensuring that every visit is a celebration. Come share our love for good food, great drinks, and unforgettable memories!

Our History

Good Food!

At SweetSugaWingz, every bite is a taste of paradise! Our wings are the stars of the menu, from the sweet heat of Mango Habanero to the irresistible crunch of Lemon Pepper Dry Rub, all cooked to crispy perfection and dripping with flavor. Pair them with our mouthwatering coconut shrimp or loaded beach nachos for the ultimate seaside feast. Satisfy your cravings with juicy island-style burgers, fresh tropical salads, and shareable platters, perfect for groups. Wash it all down with ice-cold beers or a frozen margarita as you soak up the beach vibes. At Wings & Waves, it's always a flavor-packed escape!
